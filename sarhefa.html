Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs)

STIs (Sexually Transmitted Infections) are infections you can get through sexual contact, like vaginal, anal, or oral sex. Lots of people get STIs, and it's nothing to be ashamed of. Many STIs don't even cause noticeable symptoms, so you might not know you have one. That's why it's important to get tested if you're sexually active.
